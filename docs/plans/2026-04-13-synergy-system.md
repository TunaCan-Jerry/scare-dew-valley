# Scaredew Valley — Character Synergy System

## Overview

Every character has:
1. **Archetype combo** (Fight+Fix, Know+Weird, etc.)
2. **Trait** (individual ability)
3. **Tags** (Divine, Technomancer, Occult, etc.) — groupings that unlock combo mechanics
4. **Synergy bonds** — specific pairings that create new effects

## Character Tags

| Tag | Description | Characters |
|-----|-------------|------------|
| **Divine** | Gods, spirits, or conduits of divine power | Ohm (conditional), Hearth Spirit, Coyote, Vess, Cheshire |
| **Technomancer** | Tech ability that borders on supernatural | Ohm, Pax, Candice |
| **Occult** | Supernatural knowledge or practice | Everett, Mabel, Vess, Rem (seed-dependent: only if theology or folklore interest) |
| **Military** | Formal combat training | Sal, Jasper, Marco |
| **Outsider** | Non-human or from outside normal reality | Deep One, Coyote, Cheshire, Hearth Spirit |
| **Caretaker** | Primarily keeps people going emotionally/physically | Greg, Marco, Mabel |
| **Maker** | Builds, forges, or creates physical things | Rowan, Pax, Candice, Ohm |

## Tag Synergies

Triggered when 2+ characters sharing a tag are on the farm.

### Technomancer (2+)
- **Tech Network** — all turrets share targeting data. No redundant shots. Effective DPS ~+30%.
- **3 Technomancers: Automated Grid** — machines operate without power for one night per run.

### Occult (2+)
- **Ritual Circle** — unlock group rituals at Arcane Study. Mass ward, area cleanse, enemy weakness amplifier.
- **3+ Occult: Deep Research** — final arcane study tier. Reveals source of the apocalypse.

### Military (2+)
- **Fire Team** — +15% accuracy, covering fire (nearby military auto-assist when one is attacked).
- **3 Military: Coordinated Assault** — once per night, all military focus fire one target. Devastating burst.

### Divine (2+)
- **Pantheon** — divine cutscenes. Harder nights (divine presence attracts stronger enemies). One combined divine ability per night.
- **3+ Divine: Convergence** — reality warps. Farm becomes nexus. Final boss variant changes. Secret ending.

### Outsider (2+)
- **Strange Attractor** — unusual enemies appear more often. But unusual loot drops more — rare items, unique blueprints.
- **3+ Outsider: Thin Veil** — non-corporeal enemies damageable by normal means, but corporeal enemies gain spectral abilities.

### Caretaker (2+)
- **Safety Net** — morale floor at 25%. Characters stabilize instead of breaking down.
- **3 Caretaker: Unbreakable Spirit** — once per run, full morale restoration after devastating night.

### Maker (2+)
- **Workshop Synergy** — build speed +20%. Repair costs reduced. New combined build options.
- **3+ Maker: Masterwork Forge** — once per run, build one indestructible structure.

## Pillar Synergies

Based on archetype pillar representation on the farm.

| Condition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| All 5 pillars represented | **Balanced Community** — +5% all stats globally |
| 3+ of same pillar | **Specialization** — that pillar's bonuses +20% |
| No Fighter | **Vulnerable** — night defense -15% |
| No Community | **Fractured** — morale decays 2x faster |
| No Knowledge | **Ignorant** — no research, enemies unidentified |
| No Fixer | **Crumbling** — repair costs doubled |
| No Weird | **Mundane** — no arcane defenses, but enemy supernatural abilities also weakened |
